About Ambattur Roofings

Premium Manufacturer of Roofing Sheets & Accessories Since 2016

We are engaged in AMBATTUR ROOFINGS as a manufacturer and supplier of Profile Roofing Sheets and Roof Accessories since 2016. Our wide range of products includes:

  • Pre-Painted Colour Coated Profile Roofing Sheets
  • Crimp Curve Roofing Sheets
  • Profile Ridge, Gutter, and Barge Cap
  • Polycarbonate Profile Sheets
  • Turbo Ventilators and Hardware Items

Ambattur Roofingsspecializes in Roofing Structures, Metal Roofs, and many more support products. We are also service providers for Steel Fabrication, Erection & Roof Works.

We deliver our products with premium quality at the best prices. Our motto is to satisfy customers with quality work and complete projects within the time frame at normal transport costs. Our products are attractive, easy to maintain, and offer design flexibility for any construction need - whether home, office, or factory. Made using the latest technology, they match international standards. Customers can choose colors and specifications to get the best possible product at cost-effective prices.
